Characters from Overlord

What is Overlord?

“Overlord” is an amazing story written by the Japanese author Kugane Maruyama, later adapted into an anime and manga. The story takes place in the virtual reality game “Yggdrasil,” where the main character, a player who decides to stay in the game just before it shuts down, suddenly discovers that he and his entire guild have been transported to an unknown world.

Season One

In the first season, Ainz, formerly known as Suzuki Satoru, finds himself trapped in the world of the game Yggdrasil after its sudden shutdown. Initially, Ainz explores the new rules of this world, discovering that his powers and attributes remain intact, and that NPCs have come to life. He quickly realizes that he must develop a plan to protect the Tomb of Nazarick and conquer the surrounding world. As the plot progresses, Ainz faces various challenges from interacting with local powers and factions that see him either as a threat or an opportunity.

Season Two

In the second season, Ainz strengthens his power over Nazarick, confronting various forces and factions, including the Re-Estize Kingdom and the Holy Kingdom. His strategic actions and growing influence begin to attract interest and concerns among other powers. He implements a plan to expand Nazarick's influence, testing the loyalty of his subordinates and manipulating events from behind the scenes.

Season Three

In the third season, Ainz becomes the Sorcerer King, expands his territory, and gains recognition as an invincible leader. His alliances, intrigues, and political maneuvers are tools to strengthen his dominance and establish an order that meets his goals. New challenges and rivals appear, but with the support of his loyal guardians, Ainz moves toward absolute hegemony, showing his uncompromising attitude in achieving his goals.

Season Four

In the fourth season, recognized as the undisputed ruler, Ainz continues expanding the Sorcerer Kingdom. His political intrigues and diplomatic maneuvers become more complex as he strengthens relationships with neighboring kingdoms and enemies who perceive his growing power as a threat. Ainz faces new challenges, both internal and external, while trying to maintain Nazarick's unity and gain the support and respect of other nations. Meanwhile, new secrets and ancient forces begin to reveal themselves, forcing Ainz and his loyal guardians to make decisions that will determine the future of the entire world.

Ainz Ooal Gown

Character: cold, calculating

Race: Undead

Title: Sorcerer King

Ainz is an immortal archmage and the ruler of the Great Tomb of Nazarick. He possesses immense power and vast resources and is a master of planning and foresight, making him an exceptional leader.

Shalltear Bloodfallen

Character: fanatical, unpredictable

Race: Vampire

Title: Blood Valkyrie

Shalltear is one of the most powerful guardians, who loves and worships Ainz. She is known for her ruthless nature and unpredictable behavior in battle.

Albedo

Character: loyal, fanatical

Race: Demon

Title: Overseer of the Guardians

Albedo is the overseer of the Guardians and is obsessively in love with Ainz. She is beautiful, intelligent, and extremely loyal to her master.

Demiurge

Character: cunning, rational

Race: Demon

Title: Creator of Hellfire

Demiurge is the smartest of the Guardians, with average combat abilities. His strategic thinking and ability to predict enemy moves are unparalleled.

Mare

Character: shy, cautious

Race: Dark Elf

Title: Lord of Nature

Mare is the younger brother, who always wears female clothing. He possesses useful abilities, including nature control, which makes him key in defensive battles.

Aura

Character: energetic, confident

Race: Dark Elf

Title: Renowned Beast Trainer

Aura is the older sister and an animal trainer who often wears boys' clothes. She is brave and energetic, excelling in leadership and coordination of field activities.

Cocytus

Character: brave, enduring

Race: Insect King

Title: Creator of Frozen Rivers

Cocytus is one of the most powerful guardians, with great strength and respect for opponents. He can adapt his strategy to ensure victory.

Pandora's Actor

Character: loyal, cunning

Race: Doppelgänger

Title: Commander of Special Missions

Pandora's Actor was created by Ainz himself and possesses extraordinary adaptive abilities as well as the ability to take the forms of various beings. His loyalty is absolute, and his role is to protect the secrets of Nazarick and carry out special missions.

Narberal Gamma

Character: cold, professional

Race: Doppelgänger

Title: Member of the Pleiades

Narberal Gamma is one of the combat maids of Nazarick and a loyal member of the Pleiades. She is known for her cold attitude towards humans and exceptional magical abilities, making her an excellent support in battles.

Yuri Alpha

Character: responsible, composed

Race: Undead

Title: Leader of the Pleiades

Yuri Alpha is the leader of the battle maid group, the Pleiades. Her role is to coordinate the team's actions and maintain order. She is known for her discipline, loyalty, and calm character, making her an authority within the group.

Lupusregina Beta

Character: mischievous, loyal

Race: Werewolf

Title: Member of the Pleiades

Lupusregina Beta is one of the Pleiades maids with an extremely playful nature, often hiding her true loyalty and dedication to Nazarick. Despite her playful personality, she is extremely effective in completing her assigned missions.

CZ2128 Delta

Character: quiet, precise

Race: Automaton

Title: Sniper of the Pleiades

CZ2128 Delta is a quiet and precise member of the Pleiades, specializing in marksmanship. Her calm and measured demeanor makes her an excellent support in operations that require precision and composure.

Solution Epsilon

Character: cunning, mysterious

Race: Slime

Title: Member of the Pleiades

Solution Epsilon is one of Nazarick's combat maids, known for her infiltration abilities. She can manipulate opponents and quickly adapt to situations, making her a dangerous adversary.

Entoma Vasilissa Zeta

Character: devoted, calm

Race: Insectoid

Title: Member of the Pleiades

Entoma Vasilissa Zeta is a calm and devoted member of the Pleiades. Her abilities are based on controlling insects, which she uses for combat and espionage. Despite her gentle appearance, she is a dangerous opponent.
