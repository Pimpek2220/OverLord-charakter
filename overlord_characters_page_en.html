Overlord Character Introduction

What is Overlord?

“Overlord” is an exciting story created by the Japanese author Kugane Maruyama, later adapted into anime and manga. The story takes place in the virtual reality game “Yggdrasil”, where the protagonist decides to stay in the game before its shutdown, only to discover that he and his entire guild have been transported to an unknown world.

Season One

In the first season, Ainz (originally known as Suzuki Satoru) becomes trapped in the world of the game “Yggdrasil” after its sudden shutdown. Initially, Ainz explores the rules of this new world and finds that his powers and attributes remain intact, and the NPCs have gained awareness. He quickly realizes that he needs to create a plan to protect the Great Tomb of Nazarick and conquer the surrounding world. As events unfold, Ainz faces challenges from interacting with local powers and factions who view him as either a threat or an opportunity.

Season Two

In the second season, Ainz consolidates his control over Nazarick and confronts various powers and factions, including the Re-Estize Kingdom and the Holy Kingdom. His strategic actions and growing influence begin to attract attention and concern from other forces. He implements a plan to expand Nazarick's influence, testing the loyalty of his subordinates and manipulating events from behind the scenes.

Season Three

In the third season, Ainz becomes the Sorcerer King, expands his territory, and gains recognition as an invincible leader. His alliances, intrigues, and political maneuvers are tools to strengthen his dominion and establish an order that meets his goals. New challenges and enemies arise, but with the support of his loyal guardians, Ainz moves towards absolute hegemony, showing an uncompromising approach to achieving his aims.

Season Four

In the fourth season, Ainz, now recognized as the undisputed ruler, continues to expand the influence of the Sorcerer Kingdom. His political intrigues and diplomatic maneuvers become more complex as he strengthens relationships with neighboring kingdoms and those who see his growing power as a threat. Ainz faces new internal and external challenges, striving to keep Nazarick united and gain the support and respect of other nations. Meanwhile, new secrets and ancient forces begin to emerge, forcing Ainz and his loyal guardians to make decisions that will determine the fate of the entire world.

Ainz Ooal Gown

Personality: Calm, Calculating

Race: Undead

Title: Sorcerer King

Ainz is an immortal archmage and the ruler of the Great Tomb of Nazarick. He possesses immense power and resources, making him a master of planning and foresight, and an exceptional leader.

Shalltear Bloodfallen

Personality: Fanatical, Unpredictable

Race: Vampire

Title: Blood Warrior

Shalltear is one of the most powerful guardians who worships and admires Ainz. She is known for her ruthless nature and unpredictable behavior in battle.

Albedo

Personality: Loyal, Fanatical

Race: Demon

Title: Overseer of the Guardians

Albedo is the overseer of the guardians and harbors a deep, obsessive love for Ainz. She is beautiful, intelligent, and fiercely loyal to her master.

Demiurge

Personality: Clever, Rational

Race: Demon

Title: Creator of Hellfire

Demiurge is one of the smartest guardians, with average combat abilities. His strategic thinking and ability to anticipate enemy moves are unparalleled.

Mare

Personality: Shy, Cautious

Race: Dark Elf

Title: Nature Lord

Mare is the younger brother who often wears female clothing. He possesses useful abilities, including controlling nature, which makes him crucial in defensive battles.

Aura

Personality: Energetic, Confident

Race: Dark Elf

Title: Legendary Beast Tamer

Aura is the elder sister and a beast tamer, often dressed in boyish attire. She is brave, energetic, and excels at leading and coordinating actions on the battlefield.

Cocytus

Personality: Brave, Persistent

Race: Insectoid King

Title: Creator of Frozen Rivers

Cocytus is one of the most powerful guardians, possessing great strength and respect for his opponents. He can adapt his strategy to ensure victory.

Pandora's Actor

Personality: Loyal, Cunning

Race: Doppelgänger

Title: Special Task Commander

Pandora's Actor was created by Ainz himself and has extraordinary adaptive abilities and the power to take on the form of different beings. His loyalty is absolute, and his role is to protect Nazarick's secrets and perform special missions.

Narberal Gamma

Personality: Cold, Professional

Race: Doppelgänger

Title: Member of Pleiades

Narberal Gamma is one of Nazarick's battle maids and a loyal member of Pleiades. She is known for her cold demeanor towards humans and exceptional magical abilities, making her a valuable support in battles.

Yuri Alpha

Personality: Responsible, Composed

Race: Undead

Title: Leader of Pleiades

Yuri Alpha is the leader of the battle maid group Pleiades. Her duty is to coordinate the group's actions and maintain order. She is known for her discipline, loyalty, and calm nature, making her an authority within the group.

Lupusregina Beta

Personality: Mischievous, Loyal

Race: Werewolf

Title: Member of Pleiades

Lupusregina Beta is one of the Pleiades, known for her mischievous nature, which often hides her true loyalty and dedication to Nazarick. Despite her playful personality, she is extremely effective when carrying out her missions.

CZ2128 Delta

Personality: Quiet, Precise

Race: Automaton

Title: Pleiades Sniper

CZ2128 Delta is a member of the Pleiades specializing in marksmanship. Her calm and balanced personality makes her an excellent asset in operations requiring precision and control.

Solution Epsilon

Personality: Cunning, Mysterious

Race: Slime

Title: Member of Pleiades

Solution Epsilon is one of Nazarick's battle maids, known for her infiltration abilities. She is skilled at manipulating opponents and adapting quickly to situations, making her a formidable adversary.

Entoma Vasilissa Zeta

Personality: Loyal, Calm

Race: Insectoid

Title: Member of Pleiades

Entoma Vasilissa Zeta is a calm and devoted member of the Pleiades. Her skills are based on controlling insects, which she uses in combat and for spying. Despite her gentle appearance, she is a dangerous opponent.
